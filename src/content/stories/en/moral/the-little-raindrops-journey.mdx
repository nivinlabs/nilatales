In the land of clouds lived a tiny raindrop named Selvan. Every day he nestled in the soft, white folds of his cloud, swaying and bouncing gently. The cool wind sang him lullabies. Far away, the sun smiled down from the sky.

"How comfortable it is here!" Selvan would think. "I wonder what would happen if I ever went down to the earth below?"

One day, the cloud slowly darkened. Below, the earth came into clear view. The fields were dry and cracked. The pond had shrunk until its banks had split open. The leaves on the trees hung limp and drooping.

Just then, Selvan noticed a small flower. It was a yellow flower, but its petals were wilted and its head hung low.

"Water... just a little water..." Selvan could almost hear the flower whispering softly.

Selvan's heart trembled. "If I fall down, I won't be me anymore! I'm safe here in the cloud," he said to himself.

A big raindrop nearby spoke up: "Selvan, every drop has a journey of its own. That journey only becomes meaningful when it helps someone."

Selvan looked down again. The flower's stem drooped even further. It seemed as though its tiny life might flicker out at any moment.

"I may be just a small drop. But if I can help even one living thing, that is my greatest pride," Selvan decided.

The next moment, he gently let go of the cloud. The wind cradled him as he fell. "No fear!" Selvan encouraged himself. The warm scent of the soil rose up to welcome him.

*Plop!* Selvan landed right beside the roots of the yellow flower. The earth drank him in lovingly. Selvan sank into the soil and touched the roots.

A little while later, the flower lifted its head. Its petals spread open. The yellow colour glowed brightly once more. A bee buzzed by and circled it. "Ah! What a beautiful flower!" it hummed.

Selvan had not disappeared. He lived in the flower's smile. He lived in the petals' fragrance. He shimmered as light on the tip of a green leaf.

And in that moment, Selvan understood: **Giving yourself to others brings far greater joy than keeping yourself safe.**

In the sky, there were so many stars. Big ones, small ones, bright ones, dim ones. But one little star — his name was Dinam — was very afraid.

"The darkness is so vast!" Dinam trembled. "And I am so small!"

The big star next to him laughed. "Don't be afraid, Dinam. All you need to do is shine."

"But my light is so small!" Dinam cried.

Just then, a child below looked up at the sky. "Mother, look at that little star — how beautiful it is!"

Dinam was surprised. *They saw me? They called me beautiful?*

From that day on, Dinam was no longer afraid. Because he had learned that **the light within you is the truest courage**.

Even a small light can chase away the dark.

Vivek looks at the night sky with his little sister Kutti. The moon is gone!

Kutti is worried. "Did the moon go to sleep?" she asks.

Vivek laughs. "She'll come back tomorrow."

Kutti sleeps. In the morning she wakes up and checks — the moon is still not there!

"Anna! The moon is still sleeping!"

Vivek says, "The moon doesn't sleep in the morning — hiding is her job."

Kutti thinks. "So the moon is on a secret mission?"

**In Kutti's imagination, the moon has gone off on a secret mission** — and that is next week's story.

preReading: |
  Ask the students: "Which letter did you learn first?" Write their answers on the board.
discussion:
  - "Why did Thiruvalluvar begin with the letter 'A' (அகரம்)?"
  - "Which 'first' is important in your life? (First day of school, first friend...)"
  - "When we learn, who should we offer gratitude to?"
extensionActivity: |
  Have students draw the first letter of their name as a large illustration. On the drawing, write a short line of reverence.
assessment: |
  Students should be able to recite both lines of the couplet clearly and explain the meaning of the couplet in one sentence.

## Lesson Structure (45 minutes)

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 0–10 min | Pre-reading questions (above) |
| 10–25 min | Read the story together with audio-visual aids |
| 25–35 min | Discussion (above) |
| 35–45 min | Extension activity (illustration) |

**Assessment rubric:**

- ⭐ Has memorized the couplet completely
- ⭐ Can explain the meaning of the couplet
- ⭐ Has completed the extension illustration

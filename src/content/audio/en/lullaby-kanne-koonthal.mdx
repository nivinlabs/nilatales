A gentle lullaby — for singing to little ones at night. Two lines, repeated over and over, until the voice slowly fades.

**How to sing:**

1. Sing the first two lines in a full voice
2. At "Ammā varāvarai" — lower your voice a little
3. "Taṅkam taṅkam tūṅku" — very softly
4. Start again from the first line, letting your voice grow quieter and quieter

Each time the voice grows softer, the child's eyes grow heavier.

A soft, cotton-gentle lullaby — a song that carries the child into the world of dreams.

**How to sing:**

1. Sing softly, very softly
2. At "Tārāy tūṅku" — lower your voice even more
3. Repeat until the child's eyes close
4. The last line — whisper it, do not sing

This lullaby joins the sky and the earth — a cotton bed for the child, a sleep for the moon.
